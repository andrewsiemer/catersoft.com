Catersoft Reservation Management System was designed for the banquet/catering manager. Catersoft allows banquets, parties, etc. to be entered in our system in just minutes!
Our instruction booklet will guide you to a successful installation and will show you just how easy Catersoft is to use. Whether you have much experience with computers or you are just getting started this software is a snap! Our software looks just like all your other popular Microsoft Windows®-based software. Therefore, you already know how to use it! We have made it easy for you to use Catersoft. We think you will agree.

Features

  • Very easy to use with a very short learning curve
  • Work on multiple functions at the same time
  • Re-book parties from the past in minutes!
  • Daily schedule report for your staff that tells them just what they need to know about the day’s events including last minute comments and changes
  • Customizable menu creator. Create a function’s menu without even touching the keyboard.
  • Create “Repeating Functions” for groups or events that are booked frequently. With the click of only one button, you can have your function booked!
  • E-mail or fax function sheets while your customer is still on the telephone.
  • Handy function finder allows you to locate parties in the past and future based on different criteria.
  • Multiply function feature makes it easy to book a party over a range of dates. For example, Multiply allows you to book a party on every 1st and 3rd Monday for the next year!
  • Design form letters with drag and drop ease with the included Letter Creator. 
  • Save entire menu’s with one click. Then, print out selected menus to make menu proposals.
